<h1>Presentation of Multi-Attribute Task Battery</h1>
<center>
<p>Multi-Attribute Task Battery is a multitasking environment simulation software package, first developed in 1992 by Comstock and Arnegard.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Because it requires participants to control several tasks simultaneously, this software provides a detailed understanding of the mental processes involved in managing complex and dynamic situations.</p>
</center>
